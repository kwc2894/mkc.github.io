Mindful 
Personal Project 
Role: Designer/Tester/Test Case 
Figma Link
 
Challenge:Mental health issues plague our society - especially teens and young adults. Many do not seek professional help or any help at all. We aimed to create this application to either assist with professional help or encourage users to seek it if needed while giving them the resources to monitor themselves and look into different options for help. It is extremely important to maintain a positive approach when creating all aspects of this application as well as informing but not pressuring users to use the resources provided to get help if necessary or if they feel that they should.
Solution: We set to create a prototype of an app that not only provides the neccessary resources for those in need. But also provides an outlet to set goals and even write out throughts about how their days go as people with Social Anxiety (like myself), depression and other obstacles that weigh heavily over others way of thinking would benefit greatly from a form of organizational tracking on how they are doing and where they could go afterwards in a positive manner
DISCLAIMER: All people involved with the development and design of this prototype(including myself) are not mental health professionals as we did our best with the information at our disposal Any and all infomation /advice should be taken with careful considerations
