Markadoc 
Susarc 
Role: Designer/Developer/Researcher 
 
Challenge: With COVID-19, in-person document collaboration increased in difficulty with the increasing risk of a health situation. Document collaboration already exist however a more engaging option needed to be developed despite options already availiable but lacking an extra edge to maintain consistent communication and feedback.          
Solution: Designing and developing a product that not only maintained consistent collaboration but utilized different tools such as "Markers" so users can drag, drop and even write messages to all parties involved with the document  as well as be able to manage the groups and individuals involved to bring a new form of document collaboration. More Information can be found at (Markadoc.com)
