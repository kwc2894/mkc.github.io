Fayevorites 
Role: Co-Admin/Content manager 
 
Challenge: Voice Actress and Twitch streamer Faye Mata began to foster a community during the starting stages of the COVID-19 pandemic. For her birthday, many of the community members gathered together and planned a variety of gifts. One of which would include a public platform to showcase the actor's roles, events, news, and even any additional endeavors and information regarding the community.
Solution: Solution: Working with one of the head moderators to create a website not only for Faye herself, but the community as well titled: "The Fayevorites". This website discusses roles and news for the voice actor including many community based information such as distinguished members (referred to as "Residents") as well as The Lore, a catalogue describing various jokes and fun times that the community remembers fondly and looks back on for days to come. I was initially brought on as a content editor and managed various pages regarding the news and role posts and then was promoted to co-admin which is a individual charge of the website as a whole and the editors to take on the ropes of managing content like how I started out. For more information on the website itself. Check out (Fayevorites.com)
